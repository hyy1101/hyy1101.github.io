Mac上hive数据库的搭建

环境准备：Hadoop，scala

  1. 我的Hadoop目录：/usr/local/Cellar/hadoop/2.7.3/libexec/etc/hadoop

  2. 我的scala目录：/usr/local/share/scala

  3. 我的spark目录：/usr/local/share/spark

    ​

安装spark

1.下载spark，解压

添加文件内容为：

export JAVA_HOME=/Library/Java/JavaVirtualMachines/jdk1.8.0_121.jdk/Contents/Home
export SCALA_HOME=/User/local/share/scala
export SPARK_MASTER_IP=localhost
export SPARK_WORKER_MEMORY=1g
export HADOOP_CONF_DIR=/usr/local/Cellar/hadoop/2.7.3/libexec/etc/hadoop

配置环境变量：

添加内容

export SCALA_HOME=/usr/local/share/scala/bin

export SPARK_HOME=/usr/local/share/spark/bin

export PATH=”${HADOOP_HOME}/bin:${SCALA_HOME}/bin:${SPARK_HOME}/bin:$PATH”

安装hive

  1. 采用brew来安装：
  1. 配置环境变量

export HADOOP_HOME=/usr/local/Cellar/hadoop/2.7.3/libexec
export SCALA_HOME=/usr/local/share/scala
export SPARK_HOME=/usr/local/share/spark
export HIVE_HOME=/usr/local/Cellar/hive/2.1.0/libexec
export PATH=”${HADOOP_HOME}/bin:${HIVE_HOME}/bin:${SCALA_HOME}/bin:${SPARK_HOME}/bin:$PATH”

  1. 运行配置文件
  1. 下载mysql connector并复制到hive目录下

  1. 创建mysql metastore

    • 启动mysql：打开“系统偏好设置”，单击下端的“MySQL”图标，在“MySQL”对话框中，单击“启动MySQL服务”按钮。

    • 登陆mysql：在Finder的侧边栏中单击“应用程序”，然后在“实用工具”中，双击启动“终端”命令。在终端中输入添加MySQL路径的命令：

      PATH="$PATH":/usr/local/mysql/bin

      在终端登录到MySQL的命令如下：mysql -u root -p

      我的密码是root

  1. 配置/usr/local/Cellar/hive/2.1.0/libexec/conf里面的设置文件

cd进scripts目录：/usr/local/Cellar/hive/2.1.0/libexec/scripts，然后执行schematool -initSchema -dbType mysql
